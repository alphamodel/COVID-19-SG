NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 03 Jan 2021

                  As of 3 January 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health
(MOH) has preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.

2.              There are 35 imported cases, who had already been placed
on Stay-Home Notice or isolated upon arrival in Singapore. Amongst them,
6 are Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents, and 7 are foreign
domestic workers.

3.                We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
