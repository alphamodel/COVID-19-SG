63 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 10 Sep 2020

         As of 10 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 63 cases of COVID-19 infection
in Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are 2 cases in
the community, of whom 1 is a Singaporean and 1 is a Work Pass holder.
2.      There are 6 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.

3.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
10 SEPTEMBER 2020
