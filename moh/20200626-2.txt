219 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 26 Jun 2020

1. As of 26 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 219 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 6 cases in the community, of whom 5 are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents and 1 is a Work Pass holder.
2. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
26 JUNE 2020
