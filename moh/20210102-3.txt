NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 02 Jan 2021

         As of 2 January 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.
2.       There are 33 imported cases, who had already been placed on
Stay-Home Notice or isolated upon arrival in Singapore. Amongst them, 6
are Singapore Permanent Residents, and 9 are foreign domestic workers.
3.       We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
2 JANUARY 2021
