NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 26 Feb 2021

1.      As of 26 February 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.

2.      There are 13 imported cases, who had already been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
