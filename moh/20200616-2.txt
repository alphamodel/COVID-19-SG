151 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 16 Jun 2020

1.     As of 16 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 151 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 2 cases in the community, both of whom are Work Pass holders. There
are no Singaporean/ Permanent Resident cases.

2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
