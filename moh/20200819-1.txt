93 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 19 Aug 2020

                As of 19 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 93 cases of COVID-19 infection
in Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders
currently under quarantine. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 2 cases in the community, both of whom are Work Pass holders.
2.  In addition, there are 6 imported cases, who had all been
placed on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
19 AUGUST 2020
