Minister of Health Mr Ong Ye Kung to Visit Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia



MINISTER OF HEALTH MR ONG YE KUNG TO VISIT KUALA LUMPUR, MALAYSIA


Published Date: 22 Mar 2022

1.     Mr Ong Ye Kung, Minister for Health, will make a working visit to
Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, from 23 to 25 March 2022, at the invitation of
Minister of Health of Malaysia, Yang Berhormat Tuan Khairy Jamaluddin.

2.     Mr Ong will meet with other political leaders in Malaysia during
the trip. He will also visit the National Institutes of Health on 24
March, where he will deliver a keynote address.

3.     He will be accompanied by senior officials from the Ministry of
Health.
