31 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 25 Aug 2020

1.      As of 25 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 31 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are no cases in the
community.
2.  There is 1 imported case, who had been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
