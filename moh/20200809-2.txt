175 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 09 Aug 2020

        As of 9 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 175 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders currently
under quarantine and workers tested in previous days due to dormitory
clearance. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1 case in the
community, who is a Singaporean.
2.     In addition, there are 3 imported cases, who had all been placed
on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
9 AUGUST 2020
