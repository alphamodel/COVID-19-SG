60 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 26 Aug 2020

         As of 26 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 60 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are 3 cases in the
community, of whom 1 is a Singaporean and 2 are Work Pass holders.
2.     There are 10 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
26 AUGUST 2020
