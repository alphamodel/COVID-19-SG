242 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 07 Aug 2020

                  As of 7 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health
(MOH) has preliminarily confirmed an additional 242 cases of COVID-19
infection in Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit
holders residing in foreign worker dormitories. Based on our
investigations so far, there is 1 case in the community, who is a
Singaporean.
2. In addition, there are 6 imported cases, who had all
been placed on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3. We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
7 AUGUST 2020
