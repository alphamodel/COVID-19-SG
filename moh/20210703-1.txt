4 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 03 Jul 2021

        As of 3 July 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 4 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection, all of whom are linked to previous cases, and have already
been placed on quarantine. There are no unlinked cases today.
2.     In addition, there are 3 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
1 was detected upon arrival in Singapore, while 2 developed the illness
during SHN or isolation.
3.     In total, there are 7 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.
4.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
3 JULY 2021
