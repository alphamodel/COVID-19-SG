12 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 04 Oct 2020

         As of 4 October 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 12 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are 2 cases in the
community, both of whom are Work Pass holders.
2.       There are 6 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.       We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
4 OCTOBER 2020
