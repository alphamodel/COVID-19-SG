213 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 28 Jun 2020

            As of 28 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 213 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 11 cases in the community, of whom 6 are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents and 5 are Work Pass holders.
2.      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
28 JUNE 2020
