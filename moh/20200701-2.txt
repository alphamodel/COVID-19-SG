215 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 01 Jul 2020

               As of 1 July 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 215 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 10 cases in the community, of whom 6 are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents and 4 are Work Pass holders. In addition, there are 4 imported
cases, all of whom had been placed on Stay-Home Notice or isolated upon
arrival in Singapore.



2.  We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
