7 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 06 Oct 2020

       As of 6 October 2020, 12 pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 7 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. Based on our investigations so far, 2 are in the community,
and 5 reside in dormitories.
2.   In addition, there are 4 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. In total, there are 11 new
cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore today.
3.   We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
6 OCTOBER 2020
