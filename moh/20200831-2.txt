41 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 31 Aug 2020

              As of 31 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 41 cases of COVID-19 infection
in Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are 3 cases in
the community, all of whom are Work Pass holders.

2.          There are 7 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.

3.          We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
