18 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION (20 SEP)


Published Date: 20 Sep 2020

        As of 20 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 18 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1 case in the
community, who is a Work Pass holder.

2      There are 4 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.





MINISTRY OF HEALTH
