51 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 24 Aug 2020

1. As of 24 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 51 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1 case in the
community, who is a Singaporean.
2. There are 7 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
24 AUGUST 2020
