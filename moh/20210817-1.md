    <body><h1>Update On Local Covid-19 Situation and Vaccination Progress (17 August 2021)</h1>
    <p>Published Date: 17 Aug 2021</p> <strong>Summary of local situation&nbsp;</strong><br><br>• As of 17 August 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has confirmed and verified that there are 52 cases of locally transmitted COVID-19 infection and 4 imported cases.&nbsp;<br><br>• 404 cases are currently warded in hospital. Most are well and under observation. There are currently 30 cases of serious illness requiring oxygen supplementation, and 6 in critical condition in the intensive care unit (ICU).<br><br>• Over the last 28 days, the percentage of unvaccinated who became severely ill or died is 9.6%, while that for the fully vaccinated is 1.4%.<br><br>• As of 16 August 2021, 76% of our population has completed their full regimen/ received two doses of COVID-19 vaccines, and 82% has received at least one dose.<br><div><br><strong>Update on vaccination progress</strong><br><br>As of 16 August 2021, we have administered a total of 8,464,972 doses of COVID-19 vaccines under the national vaccination programme (Pfizer-BioNTech Comirnaty and Moderna), covering 4,440,268 individuals, with 4,155,680 individuals having completed the full vaccination regimen.&nbsp;<br><br>2. In addition, 151,089 doses of other vaccines recognised in the World Health Organization’s Emergency Use Listing (WHO EUL) have been administered as of 16 August 2021, covering 83,968&nbsp;individuals.
