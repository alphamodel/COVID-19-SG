218 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION (22 JUNE 2020)


Published Date: 22 Jun 2020

     As of 22 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 218 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there is
1 case in the community who is a Work Pass holder. There are no
Singaporean/ Permanent Resident cases.
2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
22 JUNE 2020
