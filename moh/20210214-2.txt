NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 14 Feb 2021

        As of 14 February 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.
2.     There are 14 imported cases, of whom 13 had already been placed
on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. The remaining case tested
negative for COVID-19 upon arrival in Singapore on 10 February for a
work project here, and was on a controlled itinerary during his stay. He
subsequently tested positive for COVID-19 infection on 12 February as
part of our regular testing of such travellers.
3.       We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
14 FEBRUARY 2021
