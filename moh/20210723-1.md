    <body><h1>130 New Cases of Locally Transmitted Covid-19 Infection</h1>
    <p>Published Date: 23 Jul 2021</p> <p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: Arial;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; As of 23 July 2021, 12pm, </span><span style="font-size: 16px; text-align: justify; font-family: Arial;">the Ministry of Health (MOH) has preliminarily confirmed 130 new&nbsp;</span><span style="text-align: justify;"><font face="Arial" size="3">cases of locally transmitted COVID-19 infection, of which 78 are linked to the Jurong Fishery Port cluster and 6 belong to&nbsp;the KTV cluster.
