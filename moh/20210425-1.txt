NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 25 Apr 2021

       As of 25 April 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.
2.    There are 40 imported cases, who had already been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. Amongst them, 12 are
Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents, and 6 are foreign
domestic workers.
3.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
25 APRIL 2021
