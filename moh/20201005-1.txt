6 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 05 Oct 2020

           As of 5 October 2020, 12 pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminary confirmed 6 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. Based on our investigations so far, 1 is in the community,
and 5 reside in dormitories.
2.          In addition, there is 1 imported case, who had been placed
on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.           We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
