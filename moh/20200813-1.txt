102 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 13 Aug 2020

1.  As of 13 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 102 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders currently
under quarantine. Based on our investigations so far, there are 5 cases
in the community, of whom 1 is a Singapore Permanent Resident and 4 are
Work Pass holders.
2.  In addition, there are 6 imported cases, who had all been placed
on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
