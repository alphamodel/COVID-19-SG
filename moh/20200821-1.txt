117 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 21 Aug 2020

As of 21 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 117 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are 6 cases in the
community, of whom 3 are Singaporeans/ Permanent Residents and 3 are
Work Pass holders.
2. In addition, there are 13 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
21 AUGUST 2020
