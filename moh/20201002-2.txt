10 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 02 Oct 2020

      As of 2 October 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 10 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1 case in the
community, who is a Work Pass holder.
2.    There are 5 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.    We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH
2 OCTOBER 2020
