    <body><h1>2 New Cases of Locally Transmitted Covid-19 Infection</h1>
    <p>Published Date: 08 Jan 2021</p> <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-align: justify;"> 1. As of 8 January 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has preliminarily confirmed 2 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19 infection. Based on our investigations so far, the cases are in the community and had been placed on quarantine earlier. There are no new cases in the dormitories. 
<br>
<br>2.	In addition, there are 21 imported cases, who had already been placed on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. In total, there are 23 new cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore today. 
<br>
<br>3.	We are still working through the details of the cases, and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued tonight.
