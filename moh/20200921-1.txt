31 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION (21 SEP)


Published Date: 21 Sep 2020

          As of 21 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 31 cases of COVID-19 infection
in Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are no cases in
the community.

2.  There are 9 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.

3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH
21 SEPTEMBER 2020
