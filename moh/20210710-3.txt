NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 10 Jul 2021

      As of 10 July 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed zero new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection.

2.    There are 6 imported cases, who have already been placed on
Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore. 3 were
detected upon arrival in Singapore, while 3 developed the illness during
SHN or isolation.
3.    We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH
10 JULY 2021
