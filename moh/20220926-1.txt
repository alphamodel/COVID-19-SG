HONOURING OUTSTANDING EFFORTS OF HEALTHCARE STAFF AT THE NATIONAL MEDICAL EXCELLENCE AWARDS 2022


Published Date: 26 Sep 2022

1.     Five individuals and two teams received the National Medical
Excellence Awards (NMEA) this year, for excellence in the areas of care
quality, patient safety, research, as well as education and training. Mr
Ong Ye Kung, Minister for Health, gave out the awards at the NMEA
ceremony held on 26 September 2022.

2.     This annual national award recognises the efforts of outstanding
clinicians, clinician scientists and other healthcare professionals for
their contributions. It acknowledges their achievements in advancing
healthcare, improving the standards of patient safety and driving
research and education, which ultimately improve people's lives.
