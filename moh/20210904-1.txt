MINISTER FOR HEALTH MR ONG YE KUNG TO ATTEND THE G20 HEALTH MINISTERS' MEETING IN ROME, ITALY


Published Date: 04 Sep 2021

Mr Ong Ye Kung, Minister for Health, will attend the G20 Health
Ministers' Meeting (HMM) in Rome, Italy, from 5 to 6 September 2021.
2. The G20 HMM is an annual meeting comprising of 19 countries and the
EU, invited countries, as well as several international and regional
organisations. Singapore is attending the G20 HMM as a guest country at
the invitation of Italy, the current G20 presidency.
3. The G20 HMM will discuss global health issues, including addressing
the COVID-19 pandemic's impact on the Sustainable Development GoalsÂ¹
(SDGs), improving preparedness against future health emergencies, and
defining global strategies to increase the development and equitable
access of vaccines, therapeutics and diagnostics.
4. Mr Ong will meet with his counterparts at the Meeting. He will be
accompanied by senior officials from the Ministry of Health.
[1] The Sustainable Development Goals are the blueprint to achieve a
better and more sustainable future for all. They address the global
challenges we face, including poverty, inequality, climate change,
environmental degradation, peace and justice.
