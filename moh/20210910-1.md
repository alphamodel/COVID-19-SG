    <p>Published Date: 10 Sep 2021</p> <strong>Summary of local situation&nbsp;</strong><br><br>• 689 cases are currently warded in hospital. There are currently 25 cases of serious illness requiring oxygen supplementation, and 6 in critical condition in the intensive care unit (ICU).<br><br>• Over the last 28 days, the percentage of unvaccinated who became severely ill or died is 4.5%, while that for the fully vaccinated is 0.7%.<br><br>• As of 9 September 2021, 81% of our population has completed their full regimen/ received two doses of COVID-19 vaccines, and 83% has received at least one dose.&nbsp;<br><br>• As of 10 September 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has detected a total of 573 new cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore today.&nbsp;<br><div><br></div><strong>Condition of hospitalised cases</strong><br><br>As of 10 September 2021, 12pm, 689 COVID-19 cases are currently warded in hospital. Most are well and under observation. There are currently 25 cases requiring oxygen supplementation, and 6 in critical condition in the ICU. Of those who have fallen very ill, 27 are seniors above 60 years.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><br>2. Case 72986, an 80 year-old male Singaporean, has passed away from complications due to COVID-19 infection on 10 September 2021. He had developed symptoms on 4 September, and tested positive for COVID-19 infection on 9 September after being admitted to the National Centre for Infectious Diseases. He had been partially vaccinated against COVID-19, and had a history of diabetes mellitus, heart failure, hypertension and hyperlipidaemia.
