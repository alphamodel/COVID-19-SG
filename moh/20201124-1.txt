NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 24 Nov 2020

      As of 24 November 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.
2.   There are 18 imported cases, who had already been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. Amongst them, 2 are
Singaporeans and 14 are Work Pass holders who are currently employed in
Singapore, including 13 foreign domestic workers.
3.   We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
24 NOVEMBER 2020
