2 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 20 Oct 2020

        As of 20 October 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 2 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. Based on our investigations so far, both reside in
dormitories and there are no new cases in the community.
2.      In addition, there are 4 imported cases, who had all been placed
on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. In total, there are 6 new
cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore today.
3.      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
20 OCTOBER 2020
