19 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 14 Jun 2021

     As of 14 June 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 19 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. 17 are linked to previous cases, amongst whom 13 have already
been placed on quarantine, and 4 were detected through surveillance. The
remaining 2 cases are currently unlinked. Amongst them, 18 are in the
community, and 1 resides in a dormitory.
2.   In addition, there are 6 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. Of these, 3 are
returning Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents.
3.   In total, there are 25 new cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore
today.
4.   We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.





MINISTRY OF HEALTH
14 JUNE 2021
