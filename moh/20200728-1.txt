359 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 28 Jul 2020

    As of 28 July 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 359 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there is
1 case in the community, who is a Work Pass holder.
2.    In addition, there are 2 imported cases, both of whom had been
placed on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.    We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
28 JULY 2020
