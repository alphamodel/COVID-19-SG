SINGAPORE AND CHILE SIGN MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING TO STRENGTHEN COOPERATION IN AGEING ISSUES


Published Date: 15 Nov 2021

          Singapore Minister for Health, Mr Ong Ye Kung, and Chile
Minister of Social Development and Family, Dr Karla Rubilar, signed a
Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) on cooperation between the two
countries on issues related to ageing during a virtual signing ceremony
on 15 November 2021.
