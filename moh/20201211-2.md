We will also conduct serological tests for the close contacts to determine if the cases could have been infected by them.<br><br><strong>Update on condition of confirmed cases<br><br></strong>5.<strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</strong>4 more cases of COVID-19 infection have been discharged from hospitals or community isolation facilities. Of these, most are stable or improving, and none is in the intensive care unit. 55 are isolated and cared for at community facilities.
