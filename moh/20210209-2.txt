NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 09 Feb 2021

1.      As of 9 February 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.
2.  There are 11 imported cases, of whom 10 had already been placed
on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore. The remaining case is a
cabin crew with Singapore Airlines who was on a turnaround flight and
had not disembarked from the aircraft at the overseas destination. She
developed symptoms a few days after returning to Singapore.
3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
