257 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 19 Jul 2020

1. As of 19 July 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 257 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 8 cases in the community, of whom 2 are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents and 6 are Work Pass holders.
2. In addition, there are 5 imported cases who had been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3. We are still working through the details of the cases today, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
19 JULY 2020
