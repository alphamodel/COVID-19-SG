100 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 18 Aug 2020

               As of 18 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 100 cases of COVID-19
infection in Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit
holders currently under quarantine. Based on our investigations so far,
there is 1 case in the community, who is a Singaporean.

2.  In addition, there are 2 imported cases, who had both
been placed on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.

3.  We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
