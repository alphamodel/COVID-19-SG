91 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 17 Aug 2020

          As of 17 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 91 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders currently
under quarantine. Based on our investigations so far, there are no cases
in the community.
2.       There are 6 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.       We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
17 AUGUST 2020
