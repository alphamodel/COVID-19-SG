NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION (17 NOV)


Published Date: 17 Nov 2020

As of 17 November 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.
There are 6 imported cases, who had already been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
