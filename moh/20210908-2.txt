347 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 08 Sep 2021

        As of 8 September 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 347 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. Amongst the cases are 3 seniors above 70 years who are
unvaccinated/ partially vaccinated, and are at risk of serious illness.
2.     In addition, there are 2 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
3.     In total, there are 349 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.
4.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
8 SEPTEMBER 2021
