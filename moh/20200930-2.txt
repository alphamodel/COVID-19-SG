23 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 30 Sep 2020

1.      As of 30 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 23 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are 3 cases in the
community, all of whom are Work Pass holders.
2.  There are 4 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
