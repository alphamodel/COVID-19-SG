6 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 03 Oct 2020

          As of 3 October 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 6 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1 case in the
community, who is a Singaporean.

2.  There are 4 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.

3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH
3 OCTOBER 2020
