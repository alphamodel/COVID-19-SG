12 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 23 Sep 2020

              As of 23 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health
(MOH) has preliminarily confirmed an additional 12 cases of COVID-19
infection in Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1
case in the community, who is a Work Pass holder.

2.  There are 4 imported cases, who had all been placed on
Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.

3.  We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
