47 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 08 Sep 2020

        As of 8 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 47 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are no cases in the
community.
2     There is 1 imported case, who had been placed on Stay-Home Notice
upon arrival in Singapore.
3     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
