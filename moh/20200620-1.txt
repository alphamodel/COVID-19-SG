218 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 20 Jun 2020

               As of 20 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 218 cases of COVID-19
infection in Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit
holders residing in foreign worker dormitories. Based on our
investigations so far, there are 2 cases in the community, both of whom
are Work Pass holders. There are no Singaporean/ Permanent Resident
cases.
2.          We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.



MINISTRY OF HEALTH
20 JUNE 2020
