506 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 30 May 2020

          As of 30 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 506 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are five cases in the community, of whom two are Singaporeans/Permanent
Residents and three are Work Pass holders.

2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
30 MAY 2020
