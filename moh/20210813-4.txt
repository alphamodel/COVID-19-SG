45 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 13 Aug 2021

        As of 13 August 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 45 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. 20 are linked to previous cases, and have already been placed
on quarantine. 12 are linked to previous cases and were detected through
surveillance. 13 are currently unlinked. Amongst the cases are 3 seniors
above 70 years who are unvaccinated or partially vaccinated, and are at
risk of serious illness.
2.     In addition, there are 4 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
2 were detected upon arrival in Singapore, while 2 developed the illness
during SHN or isolation.
3.      In total, there are 49 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.
4.      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
13 AUGUST 2021
