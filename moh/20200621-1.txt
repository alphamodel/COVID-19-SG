262 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 21 Jun 2020

As of 21 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarilyconfirmed an additional 262 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vastmajority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories.Based on our investigations so far, there are
9 cases in the community, of whom 3are Singaporeans/Permanent Residents
and 6 are Work Pass holders.
2.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates willbe shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
