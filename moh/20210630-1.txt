5 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 30 Jun 2021

      As of 30 June 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 5 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection, all of whom are linked to previous cases, and have already
been placed on quarantine. There are no unlinked cases today.
2.    In addition, there are 11 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
6 were detected upon arrival in Singapore, while 5 developed the illness
during SHN or isolation.
3.    In total, there are 16 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.
4.    We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
30 JUNE 2021
