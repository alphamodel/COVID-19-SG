18 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 17 Sep 2020

1. As of 17 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 18 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1 case in the
community, who is a Work Pass holder.
2. There are 2 imported cases, who had been placed on Stay-Home Notice
upon arrival in Singapore.
3. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
17 SEPTEMBER 2020
