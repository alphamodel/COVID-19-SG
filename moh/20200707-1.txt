157 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 07 Jul 2020

1. As of 7 July 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 157 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there
are 20 cases in the community, of whom 12 are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents and 8 are Work Pass holders. From our preliminary
investigations, of these 20 cases, 12 were close contacts of earlier
cases, and had already been placed on quarantine. Epidemiological
investigations are ongoing for the other cases.
2. In addition, there are 3 imported cases, all of whom had been placed
on Stay-Home Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
7 JULY 2020
