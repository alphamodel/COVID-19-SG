34 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 05 Sep 2020

1. As of 5 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 34 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there are 3 cases in the
community, of whom 1 is a Singaporean and 2 are Work Pass holders.
2. There are 2 imported cases, both of whom had been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
5 SEPTEMBER 2020
