8 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 12 Jul 2021

          As of 12 July 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 8 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. 5 are linked to previous cases, and have already been placed
on quarantine. 3 are currently unlinked.



2.  In addition, there are 18 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
13 were detected upon arrival in Singapore, while 5 developed the
illness during SHN or isolation.



3.  In total, there are 26 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.



4.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.





MINISTRY OF HEALTH
