2 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 10 Nov 2020

        As of 10 November 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 2 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. Based on our investigations so far, 1 is in the community,
and 1 resides in a dormitory.
2.    In addition, there are 7 imported cases, who had already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice or isolated upon arrival in Singapore. In
total, there are 9 new cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore today.
3.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
10 NOVEMBER 2020
