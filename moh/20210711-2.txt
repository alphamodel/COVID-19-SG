1 NEW CASE OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 11 Jul 2021

      As of 11 July 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 1 new case of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection, who is currently unlinked.
2.   There are 7 imported cases, who have already been placed on
Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore. 1 was
detected upon arrival in Singapore, while 6 developed the illness during
SHN or isolation.
3.   In total, there are 8 new cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore
today.
4.   We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
