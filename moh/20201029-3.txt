NO NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION (29 OCT)


Published Date: 29 Oct 2020

1.      As of 29 October 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed that there are no new cases of locally
transmitted COVID-19 infection.
2.      There are 7 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.





MINISTRY OF HEALTH
29 OCTOBER 2020
