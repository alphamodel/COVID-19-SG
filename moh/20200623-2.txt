119 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 23 Jun 2020

As of 23 June 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has preliminarily
confirmed an additional 119 cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore,
the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in foreign
worker dormitories. Based on our investigations so far, there are 3
cases in the community, of whom 1 is a Singapore Permanent Resident and
2 are Work Pass holders.

2.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH
23 JUNE 2020
