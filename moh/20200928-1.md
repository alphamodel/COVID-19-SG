    <p>Published Date: 28 Sep 2020</p> <p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; As of 28 September 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has preliminarily confirmed an additional 15 cases of COVID-19 infection in Singapore.
