29 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 19 Aug 2021

         As of 19 August 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 29 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. 11 are linked to previous cases, and have already been placed
on quarantine. 5 are linked to previous cases and were detected through
surveillance. 13 are currently unlinked.
2.      In addition, there are 3 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
1 was detected upon arrival in Singapore, while 2 developed the illness
during SHN or isolation.
3.      In total, there are 32 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.
4.      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
19 AUGUST 2021
