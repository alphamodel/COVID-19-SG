87 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 23 Aug 2020

      As of 23 August 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 87 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore. Based on our investigations so far, there is 1 case in the
community, who is a Work Pass holder.
2.     There are 13 imported cases, who had all been placed on Stay-Home
Notice upon arrival in Singapore.
3.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
23 AUGUST 2020
