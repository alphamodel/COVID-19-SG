2 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 26 Apr 2021

            As of 26 April 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 2 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. Based on our investigations so far, 1 is in the community,
and 1 resides in a dormitory.

2.  In addition, there are 43 imported cases, who had already
been placed on Stay-Home Notice or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
Amongst them, 13 are Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents.

3.  In total, there are 45 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.

4.  We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
