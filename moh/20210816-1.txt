48 NEW CASES OF LOCALLY TRANSMITTED COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 16 Aug 2021

        As of 16 August 2021, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed 48 new cases of locally transmitted COVID-19
infection. 32 are linked to previous cases, and have already been placed
on quarantine. 7 are linked to previous cases and were detected through
surveillance. 9 are currently unlinked.
2.     In addition, there are 5 imported cases, who have already been
placed on Stay-Home Notice (SHN) or isolated upon arrival in Singapore.
All were detected upon arrival in Singapore.
3.      In total, there are 53 new cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore today.
4.      We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
16 AUGUST 2021
